A. Name of Project:

Deep Fried Tonkatsu

B. Objectives: To be able to—

  1. assemble all needed ingredients systematically;
  2. follow the correct procedures in cooking deep fried tonkatsu; and
  3. evaluate the cooked product correctly.

C. Illustration:

D. Tools and Equipments

  • non-stick pan
  • kitchen knives
  • measuring cups
  • measuring spoons
  • meat hammer

E. Ingredients:

  1. For Tonkatsu:
  • 3 pcs. Pork chops
  • 1 T. salt
  • ½ T. ground black pepper
  • 2 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cup Bread Crumbs
  • 3 cups cooking oil
  1. For Sauce:
  • 2 T. ketchup
  • 1 T. Oyster Sauce
  • 2 T. Soy Sauce
  • 5 t. Sugar
  • 2 t. vinegar
  1. For Plating
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 cups cold rice
  • 1 sachet UFC Funchow
  • 2 eggs

F. Procedures:

  1. Prepare all the needed ingredients and the tools and equipments needed.
  2. Prepare the meat by cutting the skin and bones.
  3. Pound the meat using a meat tenderizing tool to flatten it a little.
  4. Season the meat with salt and pepper.
  5. Dredge the chops on the flour then on the beaten egg mixture.
  6. Cover the meat with bread crumbs.
  7. In a non-stick pan, deep fry the already covered meat until golden brown.
  8. Once cooked, let it cool down.
  9. Cut the tonkatsu into pieces.
  10. Add the fried rice first on the plate.
  11. Consecutively, add the tonkatsu, shredded cabbage, tomatoes, and cucumber on the side.
  12. Add sauce on top of the tonkatsu and a little on the rice.
  13. Share and Enjoy!
